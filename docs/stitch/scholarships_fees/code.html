Common Questions

Is the hostel fee mandatory? expand_more

No, hostel facilities are optional. Students living within a 20km radius of the campus may opt-out or choose our dedicated transport fleet.

Can I combine multiple scholarships? expand_more

Students are typically eligible for one institutional scholarship per year. The higher of the qualifying amounts will be applied to the fee account.

Are there sibling discounts? expand_more

Yes, NIET offers a 10% loyalty discount on tuition fees for siblings currently enrolled in any of our programs.
